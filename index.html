Howwe work

STEP 1:
Just give us a call and we will email and text you the Menu. Let us know what dishes you would like to have and the event that we will be preparing for. Be it a Birthday party or even just a personal family dinner, Crumbcook will provide you with the chefs! We currently cater for upto 60 people.

Step 2:
Our team will visit the venue atleast 3-4 days prior to the event to check if the working conditions are appropriate.





STEP 3:
On the day of the event our chefs will arrive with all the ingredients. You can either sit back and relex or better, learn how to cook for free from the pros.Talk about killing two birds with one stone! .
